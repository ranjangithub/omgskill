# 🏦 Banking & Financial Services — AI Resources

## AI & Your Industry

Banking and financial services are simultaneously the most data-rich and most heavily regulated environment for AI deployment. In 2026, AI is live inside fraud detection (card transaction screening in milliseconds), credit underwriting (alternative data scoring is now mainstream at challenger banks), and trade surveillance. The challenge for institutions isn't access to AI — it's navigating the SR 11-7 model risk management framework, the OCC's updated supervisory guidance, and the Fed's emerging expectations around AI explainability while still moving fast enough to compete with fintechs who operate outside those guardrails. The institutions winning right now are those that built AI governance infrastructure early and can run model validation on a 6-week cycle instead of 6 months.

### What's actually changing

- JPMorgan Chase, Goldman Sachs, and Bank of America each report AI saving 100,000+ developer hours annually; generative AI for code, documentation, and client communication is now standard infrastructure
- The OCC's 2025 Model Risk Management update explicitly extends SR 11-7 governance requirements to LLMs and generative AI systems — institutions need formal validation before production deployment
- Real-time fraud detection AI is now table stakes; the competition has moved to synthetic identity detection and account takeover prevention, where transformer models outperform rule-based systems by 30–50%
- Robo-advisory AUM crossed $2 trillion globally in 2025; hybrid human-AI advisory models are the dominant growth pattern for wealth management in the $100K–$5M segment
- Alternative credit scoring (rent payments, utility history, cash flow analysis) is live at 50+ US lenders; fair lending compliance analysis of these models is an active regulatory focus area
- Basel III AI risk provisions and the EU's Digital Operational Resilience Act (DORA) both become fully enforced in 2026, adding compliance surface area for any institution with EU operations
