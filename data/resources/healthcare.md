# 🏥 Healthcare — AI Resources

## AI & Your Industry

Healthcare AI has crossed the threshold from experimental to operational. In 2026, ambient AI scribes are live in thousands of clinics, prior authorization automation is becoming a CMS-mandated standard, and agentic AI is beginning to manage multi-step clinical administrative workflows without human prompting. The deployment gap — the 95%+ of AI prototypes that never reach production — remains the defining challenge. The executives and operators who understand why AI fails at the production gate (data readiness, EHR integration, change management, and outcome measurement) will consistently outperform those still evaluating capability demos. Budget pressure from Medicare/Medicaid reimbursement uncertainty is making ROI documentation non-negotiable for every AI initiative in 2026.

### What's actually changing

- Ambient AI documentation (Nuance DAX, Suki, Abridge) is reducing physician documentation time by 2–3 hours/day in live deployments — the most measurable ROI in healthcare AI today
- CMS electronic prior authorization mandates (with Epic, Oracle Health, Cleveland Clinic as named early adopters) are setting new workflow expectations across the entire provider sector
- FDA has cleared 950+ AI/ML-enabled medical devices; radiology and cardiology AI is moving from pilot to standard of care at major health systems
- 67% of health system leaders say Medicare/Medicaid cuts exceeding 10% will freeze technology investments — making ROI documentation the gating criteria for every AI project
- LLMs are entering the EHR: Epic's AI Companion is live at 200+ health systems; ambient AI is the first horizontal deployment of LLM technology in clinical settings at scale
- 95%+ of healthcare AI prototypes never reach production — the problem is organizational readiness (data access, IT integration, change management), not model capability
