## Notes

- NAIC AI Model Bulletin (2023) is the foundational regulatory document — all state AI rules trace back to it
- Digital Insurance (dig-in.com) is the highest signal insuretech trade publication
- Claims AI is the fastest-moving area: fraud detection + automated claims are live at scale
- Watch Colorado SB 169 (algorithmic fairness in insurance) — template legislation spreading to other states
