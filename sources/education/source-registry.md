## Notes

- Academic integrity is the fastest-moving issue: watch Turnitin, GPTZero, and DOE guidance closely
- FERPA creates AI data constraints unique to education — any AI vendor in schools must comply
- EdSurge is the single most reliable independent source for EdTech AI deployments and failures
- Higher ed AI governance is nascent — university AI policies are being written in real time (2025-2026)
