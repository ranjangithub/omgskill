## Notes

- OMB M-24-10 (AI in government policy) is the operational baseline — all agency AI programs must align to it
- NIST AI RMF is the US government's preferred AI risk framework — agencies are required to use it
- GAO reports on agency AI failures are valuable negative signal — what not to repeat
- DefenseScoop covers DoD AI (autonomous systems, AI weapons) — separate from civilian agency AI
- State AI differs significantly from federal — GovTech + StateScoop cover the variation
