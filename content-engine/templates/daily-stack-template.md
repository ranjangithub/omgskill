# Daily Stack Template

The output format for a complete omgskill daily briefing.

---

## Frontmatter

```
date: YYYY-MM-DD
industry: {INDUSTRY_ID}
role: {ROLE_ID}
content_goals: [{GOAL_IDS}]
signals_count: N
articles_scanned: N
```

---

## Section 1 — Executive Summary (stay-updated)

3–5 bullets. Each bullet is one complete sentence that names the signal and the implication.
Format: **[Actor] [action] — [implication].**

Example:
- FDA clears AI prior authorization system — processing time drops from 14 days to 6 hours for 78% of residential transactions.

---

## Section 2 — Top Signals (all goals)

For each signal (3 free / up to 10 Pro):

```
### Signal {N}: {TITLE}
**Classification:** {CLASS}

**What happened:** 2–3 sentences. Facts only. No editorializing.

**Why it matters:** 1–2 sentences. The implication, not the event.

**Industry impact:** 1–2 sentences. What changes in {INDUSTRY}.

**Role impact:** 1–2 sentences. What changes for a {ROLE}.

**Expert take:** 1–2 sentences. The non-obvious read. What most people are missing.

**LinkedIn hook:** A single sentence, ready to paste as the opening line of a post. No "excited to share." No "hot take." Just the signal, stated sharply.

[If goal = create-content]
**Content angle:** Post format, angle, and hook. What to write and why it will perform.

[If goal = sell-better]
**Sales intel:** Buyer trigger, target title, conversation opener, pain point surfaced.

[If goal = learn-faster]
**Explainer:** Plain-English breakdown. Analogy, mechanism, key terms.

[If goal = lead-decide]
**Executive brief:** Strategic risk or opportunity. 90-day action. Board-ready framing.
```

---

## Section 3 — Quick Hits (stay-updated, Pro)

3–5 one-line signals with bold lead. Format:
**[Actor] [action]** — [implication in one clause].

---

## Section 4 — Content Ideas (create-content, Pro)

2–3 developed post angles with:
- Post format (list, story, data, opinion, how-to)
- Hook (first line, no "I" opener)
- Angle (what makes this differentiated from what everyone else will post)
- CTA suggestion

---

## Section 5 — Sales Intel (sell-better, Pro)

Synthesized buyer intelligence for {INDUSTRY} × {ROLE}:
- 1 buying trigger with urgency framing
- 2 conversation openers (email / LinkedIn cold open)
- 1 discovery question
- 1 objection handle

---

## Section 6 — Explainers (learn-faster, Pro)

1–2 concept explainers, each with:
- Plain-English definition
- Analogy
- Why it matters to {INDUSTRY} × {ROLE}
- 3-term glossary

---

## Section 7 — Strategy Brief (lead-decide, Pro)

For executives and decision-makers:
- Strategic risk (what to watch, what exposure exists)
- Strategic opportunity (first-mover advantage window)
- 90-day action recommendation
- Decision framework (if relevant)

---

## Section 8 — Drive Time (Pro)

5-minute radio-style briefing script. Conversational. No bullet points. Reads like a NPR segment written by a domain expert.

Format:
```
**Drive Time — [Signal Title]**

[Opening hook — 1 sentence that makes you want to keep listening]

[The signal — what happened, concisely]

[The implication — what it means for the listener specifically]

[The action — what to do with this information]

[The bottom line — 1 sentence closer]
```
